Overview
I worked with Propeller to design their brand identity and design language. The name
Propeller was inspired by the companies overall objective — to propel great business
ideas. The identity was designed with this very objective in mind. The logomark is formed
from a propeller shape to create a unique letter P. In addition to the brand I
also worked with Propeller to develop their product interface as well as their website visuals.
